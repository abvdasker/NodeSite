Ant Colony Optimization Algorithm

Download:

Basic
Visualization

Using Java, I built a visualization of the Ant Colony Optimization (ACO) metaheuristic used to solve the Generalized Assignment Problem (GAP). ACO is a metaheuristic which can be applied to NP-Hard problems to find good solutions in a reasonable amount of time, taking its inspiration from observed ant behavior; specifically, ants' use of pheromones to find short routes to food.
